What is a podcast?

A podcast is an audio program that is made available on the internet for download. Podcasts can be about any topic and can be produced by anyone with a computer and a microphone. They are often created by individuals or small groups and can be listened to on a variety of devices, including smartphones, tablets, and computers. Podcasts can be listened to live or recorded and can be either short or long form. They are becoming increasingly popular as a way for people to stay informed, entertained, and educated on a wide range of topics. Many podcasts are also available on streaming platforms like Spotify, Apple Music and more.
